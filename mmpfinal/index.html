    WHY DOES THIS HAPPEN?!

    An eclipse happens when the sun and moon allign together perfectly. Kind of like when you meet up with a friend!

    A SOLAR ECLIPSE

    A solar eclipse happens during the day! The sun's light casts a dark shadow on the moon!

    A LUNAR ECLIPSE

    A lunar eclipse happens during the night! The sun disapears! but it turns the moon red!
